🗂 Backup options
If Warung Spang Makandra is packed: Koeah or Toko Bersama West — both Indonesian, both on your Google Maps list.
If Wynand Fockink times out: The Waterhole (live music) is 10 min by bike on Korte Leidsedwarsstraat.
Café de Reiger (Jordaan brown café, on your list) works as alternate dinner on either day.
Hortus Botanicus is a 10-min walk from Brouwerij 't IJ — add it Day 1 if you leave De Pijp by 5:15pm.
Royal Palace is on Dam Square — exterior free, interior 45 min. You'll pass it biking Day 2.
